Welcome to Chess Mates a club for young mind!

We are a fun and educational chess community designed specifically for young adults aged 7-18.
Our mission is to help young players develop critical thinking, strategy and confidence through the exciting game of chess.

Our Programs

Beginer Program

Perfect for those just starting out. Learn how chess pieces move and basic rules

Intermediate Program

Take your chess skills to the next level by learning strategy and playing against others.

Advance Program

Prepare for tournaments and learn advanced techniques with experienced coaches.
